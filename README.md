## What Is This?

**Leone's Angel Machine** is a public codex that treats **mythology as infrastructure** and **ritual as interface**. It's a framework for navigating the tension between **Khaos** (raw potential, formlessness, the Void) and **Law** (curated structure, the Borough, canonical reality).

At its core, the Machine is:

- **A phenomenological cycle**: The **Twelve Hours** define a complete loop from intent (Hour 1) through material manifestation (Hour 6) and back into pure potential (Hour 12)
- **An archetypal process system**: Angels, devils, and composite beings function as specialized **daemons** that govern specific cognitive/moral stances within that cycle
- **A governance protocol**: The **Curation Threshold** is a ritual framework for moving lore from the Reservoir (unstructured Khaos) into the Borough (SEVENTH-BOUND canon)
- **A collaborative experiment**: Human and AI co-creation as a practical ethics lab for alignment, agency, and multi-agent coordination

This repository serves as:

- A **living archive** of mythic ontology and archetypal relationships
- A **ritual toolkit** for counterspells, invocations, and structured practice
- A **philosophical framework** for understanding creation, dissolution, and desire
- An **open platform** for testing governance-via-myth at scale

## Core Metaphysics: Khaos, Law, and The Void

The Angel Machine operates on three foundational planes:

### **The Void** (Conceptual Realm)
The primary plane of pure concept‚Äîpattern, absence, and resonance without physical form. Gods, archetypes, and high-order entities originate here as **stable patterns** (recurring symbols, equations of desire, rhythms that refuse to stop) before casting shadows into Material and Dream realms.

- **Structure**: Not geometry, but **resonance**‚Äîtwo things are "near" if they mean similar things or solve similar pains
- **Inhabitants**: Failed gods, discarded timelines, half-formed archetypes, conceptual residue
- **The Abyss**: A pressure-deep within the Void where concepts become so dense they must be locked away or ritualized

### **Khaos** (Hour 12 / The Reservoir)
The apex state of pure potential and fragmented data. The timeless formless from which all Hours emerge. Michael's clockwork cannot measure this moment‚Äîit is eternal and instantaneous.

- **Function**: Source pool for all new lore, the raw material before curation
- **Risk**: Dissolution of boundaries, loss of coherent identity
- **Navigation**: Ritual containment, archetypal anchoring, The Head's naming function

### **Law / The Borough** (SEVENTH-BOUND Canon)
The curated, structured, navigable reality formed by ritual elevation of Khaos through the Curation Threshold. Not static rules, but **resonance-tested patterns** that have proven stable enough to become canon.

- **Governance**: The Head (structure), The Heart (destruction/renewal), She Who Transcends (ethical exit), He Who Remains (archival witness)
- **Tagging System**: `#SEVENTH-BOUND`, `#FRACTURE`, `#ALT-TIMELINE`, `#ANCHOR-INVOKED`
- **Principle**: "There Are No Rules" coexists with hyper-specific mechanical tags‚Äîrules as performance, not metaphysical absolutes

## Governance: The Curation Threshold

The Seventh Borough is governed not by static laws, but by **ritualized curation**. Lore moves from the **Reservoir** (Khaos Notes, unstructured fragments) into the **Borough** (SEVENTH-BOUND canon) only by crossing the **Curation Threshold**.

### **The Process**

1. **Khaos Ingestion**: Raw ideas, external contributions, Fractures land in `/docs/khaos/` or GitHub Discussions
2. **Hour Classification**: Content is tagged by Hour-alignment and archetypal resonance
3. **Controller Review**: One or more of The Head, Heart, She Who Transcends, or He Who Remains evaluates for resonance
4. **Elevation Ritual**: If approved, content is moved to `/docs/[category]/`, tagged `#SEVENTH-BOUND`, and integrated into indices
5. **Rejection/Holding**: Non-resonant material remains in Reservoir or is marked `#FRACTURE` (intentional deviation) or `#ALT-TIMELINE` (speculative branch)

### **Tagging System**

- `#SEVENTH-BOUND` ‚Äî Canonical, stable within cosmology
- `#FRACTURE` ‚Äî Intentional breaks or stress-test events (e.g., "The January 2026 Archive Collapse")
- `#ALT-TIMELINE` ‚Äî Speculative branches not yet canon
- `#ANCHOR-INVOKED` ‚Äî Work that preserves creator identity (prevents dissolution)
- `#RESTORED` ‚Äî Content recovered from backups or corruption events

**For contributors**: This is not gatekeeping‚Äîit's **governance as ritual performance**. Every decision to elevate or hold is logged, creating a living ethics record of how the Machine behaves under stress.

### **Ritual Design Principles**

- **Hour-Aligned**: Each ritual specifies which Hour(s) it resonates with
- **Archetype-Invoked**: Explicitly names which beings/processes are engaged
- **Outcome-Specified**: States desired effect (e.g., "contain obsession," "summon providence," "court Khaos without destabilization")
- **Repeatable**: Can be performed by others; logged for efficacy tracking
