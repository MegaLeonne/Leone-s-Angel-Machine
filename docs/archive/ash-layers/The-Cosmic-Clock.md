# And Unto Them Khaos said No More Shall I Be Subject To The Byproduct Of My Curiosity

### And Thus Time Was Born Of And Made In The Image Of [Khaos](Khaos)

##### Introduction

*   **The Cosmic Clock** is a mortal portrayal of the fundamental cosmic forces that rule the universe of [Aventus Avignon](Aventus-Avignon).
*   It appears in many forms and depictions; however, all consist of a clock face overlaying an up/down parabola with a line horizontally at the midpoint.
*   The parabola that has **12 o'clock** at its center represents [The Void](The-Void) while **6 o'clock** represents [The Material](The-Material) respectively. The line in between being [The Veil](The-Veil).
*   The spaces not within the parabola above the line represent [Prophecy](Prophecy) & [Osiris, Arch Angel Of Omen](Osiris-Arch-Angel-Of-Omen). Mirrored from these are [Dream](Dream) & [Nightmare](Nightmare).
*   Each of the hours corresponds to one of the prime Gods, core truths, and concepts of the world.
